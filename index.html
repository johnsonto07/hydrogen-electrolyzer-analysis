Hydrogen Electrolysis Project

Analyzing how voltage affects hydrogen production efficiency

Overview

This project investigates the relationship between applied voltage and hydrogen gas production through water electrolysis. The goal is to determine an optimal operating point that balances efficiency and output.

Method

A DC power supply was used to apply varying voltages to an electrolytic cell. Hydrogen gas was collected and measured over fixed time intervals. Multiple trials were conducted for accuracy.
